Online Shopping becomes more fun

You think you can shop online with friends? Contra will make shopping online more fun than you ever imagined









How it works

Watch how our various feature mesh to create an amazing user experience.

Features

Your favourite products all at one place

Shop with ease, security and comfort. Contra provides you these at one go. With contra you can shop online, sell online. Contra also allows you to have a mobile wallet to enable you still make purchases even when your debit card is unavailable

Confused about a product? Call a friend

Go shopping with friends. You don’t have to make that shoe choice alone alone. Your messages with friends are fully encrypted, so your privacy is ensured.

Track the progress of your order

Of course, you can’t wait to get those shiny pairs of jordans. Contra allows you to track the progress of your order from placement to shipping to delivery
